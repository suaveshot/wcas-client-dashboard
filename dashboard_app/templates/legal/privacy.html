Privacy Policy

What we collect

  • Business facts — name, address, phone, services, hours, policies, pricing, FAQ, voice/tone samples. Captured during onboarding or pulled from your public website.
  • Connected accounts — OAuth refresh tokens for the services you explicitly connect (Google, Meta, etc.). Stored encrypted at rest, never shared.
  • Operational data — audit logs of which automations ran, when, and what they produced. Used to show you results + debug issues.
  • Acceptance records — when you click through terms/privacy, we record the timestamp, your IP, and a truncated user-agent for our audit trail.

What we do NOT do

  • Sell your data.
  • Share it with third parties (other than vendors you explicitly connect).
  • Train AI models on your content.
  • Read personal messages (email + Gmail scopes are filtered to business keywords).

Your rights

California + GDPR-equivalent rights: you can request a full export of everything we hold about your business at any time, or ask us to delete it. Email sam@westcoastautomationsolutions.com and we respond within 30 days.

Retention

While you're an active client, data lives as long as the automations need it. On cancellation, we export your data to you + delete after a 30-day grace window. Audit logs have a longer retention for compliance purposes (seven years for tax + legal records, stripped of personal identifiers after cancellation).

Subprocessors

We use third-party platforms to run the automations: an AI model provider (for drafting content in your voice), Google + Meta (OAuth providers you connect to directly), Airtable (internal CRM), Hostinger (hosting), Gmail + Twilio (transactional messaging). Each subprocessor is subject to its own privacy policy. Full current list available on request.
